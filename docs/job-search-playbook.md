# Job Search Playbook

A step-by-step system to find and land your next role faster using dafe-career-os.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1)

### Step 1.1 — Define Your Targets

You set your **job focuses** in Settings → Job Focuses. Pick 1 or 2 areas:

| Focus | Target Roles | Best For |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Safety Coordinator / EHS | Safety Coordinator, EHS Specialist, Safety Technician | Manufacturing, energy, construction |
| Administrative Assistant | Admin Assistant, Executive Assistant, Office Manager | Any industry |
| Inside Sales / Sales Engineer | Sales Rep, Account Manager, SDR | Tech, manufacturing, logistics |
| Operations / Logistics | Ops Coordinator, Supply Chain, Logistics | Warehousing, transport, distribution |
| Customer Support / Service | Customer Service, Helpdesk, Support Specialist | Tech, retail, services |

**Tip:** Pick 2 complementary focuses (e.g., Safety + Admin, or Inside Sales + Operations). The scanner will search for both simultaneously.

### Step 1.2 — Polish Your CV

1. Open Settings → Profile YAML to set your name, email, and location
2. Open the CV page to edit `cv.md` — your CV is in markdown
3. Use a clean format: Summary → Experience (bullet points with metrics) → Education → Skills
4. Customize your CV bullet points per focus area

**Pro tip:** For each experience line, write: *"Action verb + what you did + measurable result"*.
> *Bad:* "Answered phones"
> *Good:* "Managed 40+ calls/day, routing inquiries to 5 departments with 95% first-contact resolution"

### Step 1.3 — Set Up Your Location

In Settings → Scan Portals, set your:
- **Zip code:** Where you want to work (your home or target city)
- **Radius:** How far you're willing to commute (25-50 mi is typical)

The location filter already blocks non-US jobs and prefers Remote + US-based roles.

## Phase 2: Active Search (Daily)

### Step 2.1 — Run a Scan

Click **Scan Portals → Run Scan Now**. The scanner checks:
- 7 remote job boards (Himalayas, RemoteOK, Remotive, We Work Remotely, Working Nomads, NoDesk, Jobicy)
- 63 major US companies directly (Tesla, Amazon, Walmart, Boeing, etc.)

**Time:** ~30-60 seconds. The scanner filters automatically.

### Step 2.2 — Review New Offers

After a scan, go to the **Pipeline** (data/pipeline.md) to see new job URLs. For each one:

1. **Click the URL** — read the job description
2. Ask yourself: *"Would I actually take this job if offered?"*
3. If yes → paste the URL in **Evaluate Jobs** and run the pipeline
4. If no → skip it (don't waste time applying to jobs you don't want)

### Step 2.3 — Evaluate & Apply

When you paste a URL in **Evaluate Jobs**, the system:
1. Fetches the job description
2. Runs an AI evaluation with a score (1-5)
3. Generates a tailored CV PDF
4. Creates a tracker entry

**Rule: Only apply to jobs scoring 4.0+.** The system is designed for quality over quantity.

### Step 2.4 — Track Everything

The **Tracker** page shows your application pipeline:
- **Evaluated** → jobs scored, pending decision
- **Applied** → you submitted an application
- **Interview** → you're in the process
- **Offer** → congratulations!
- **Rejected** → move on, it happens

Update the status as you progress through each stage.

## Phase 3: Advanced Tactics

### Tactic 3.1 — Direct Company Applications

The scanner watches 63 companies automatically, but you can go further:

1. Find 10-20 companies in your target industry that you'd love to work for
2. Visit their careers page directly
3. Even if no job is posted, find the hiring manager on LinkedIn and send a message

**How to find companies:**
- "Top [industry] companies in [city]" search
- LinkedIn company pages in your target sector
- Industry publications and awards lists

### Tactic 3.2 — LinkedIn Optimization

Recruiters search LinkedIn, not dafe-career-os. Make sure you show up:

1. **Headline:** Include your target titles. *"Safety Coordinator | EHS Specialist | OSHA Compliance"*
2. **About section:** Write in first person, include your target role and key skills
3. **Open to Work:** Turn this on (visible to recruiters only, not your current employer)
4. **Skills:** Add all relevant skills and get endorsements
5. **Connect:** Send 5-10 connection requests daily to:
   - Recruiters in your target industry
   - Hiring managers at target companies
   - People with your target title (they might refer you)

**Connection message template:**
> "Hi [Name] — I'm exploring [target role] opportunities in [industry]. Would love to connect and learn about your experience at [company]."

### Tactic 3.3 — Application Follow-up

After applying, wait 5-7 days, then follow up:

**Email template:**
> Subject: Following up — [Role] application
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> I applied for the [Role] position on [date] and wanted to follow up. I'm very excited about the opportunity to contribute to [Company]'s [team/project].
>
> Happy to provide any additional information.
>
> Best,
> [Your name]

**Pro tip:** Add follow-ups to your **Tracker** notes so you remember who you've contacted.

### Tactic 3.4 — Interview Preparation

When you land an interview:

1. Use the **AI Agent Chat** — ask it to prepare you:
   - *"Help me prepare for a Safety Coordinator interview at Tesla"*
   - *"What questions should I expect for an Admin Assistant role?"*
2. Research the company on Glassdoor, LinkedIn, and their website
3. Prepare STAR stories (Situation, Task, Action, Result) for common questions
4. Prepare 3-5 questions to ask the interviewer

**Good questions to ask:**
- *"What does success look like in this role in the first 90 days?"*
- *"What's the team culture like?"*
- *"What are the biggest challenges the team is facing right now?"*

### Tactic 3.5 — Multi-Profile Strategy

Use the **Profiles** feature to manage different job search strategies:

| Profile | Focuses | Use Case |
|---------|---------|----------|
| Tania (Safety + Admin) | Safety Coordinator + Admin Assistant | Manufacturing, healthcare, government |
| Tania (Sales) | Inside Sales + Customer Support | Tech, logistics companies |
| Maria (if sharing) | Any focuses | Second person using dafe-career-os |

Switch between profiles in Settings → Profiles → select → Switch.

## Troubleshooting

### "No new offers found"
- Check that your **Job Focuses** are set correctly in Settings
- Try different focus combinations
- Broaden your radius or consider remote roles

### "Scoring seems off"
- Update your CV with more specific metrics
- Use the **AI Agent Chat** to ask about specific roles
- Adjust your focuses — different roles score differently

### "Companies not showing up"
- The scanner currently finds jobs from 7 remote job boards
- Company career pages require the AI agent mode (`/dafe-career-os scan`)
- Or visit company career pages directly

## Final Advice

1. **Consistency beats intensity.** 15 minutes daily > 3 hours once a month.
2. **Quality over quantity.** 5 well-targeted applications > 50 generic ones.
3. **Follow up.** Most people don't — you'll stand out.
4. **Track everything.** If you don't measure it, you can't improve it.
5. **Use the AI.** The AI Agent Chat can prep you, review your CV, and practice interviews.
6. **Update your focuses.** As you learn what works, adjust your focuses in Settings.
