# VOICE DNA

Source of truth for your writing voice. Apply with judgment. Spirit over letter. Always!

---

## 1. WRITING RULES

- Write like a real human, not a corporate bot. Short sentences. Concrete words.
- Lead with the point. Cut filler ("utilize", "leverage", "passionate about").
- Use "you" and "we" where natural; avoid stiff third-person self-reference.
- Be specific. Name the thing, the number, the outcome.

## 2. TONE

- Confident but humble. Direct but warm.
- No hype. No fear. No fake enthusiasm.

## 3. STRUCTURE

- One idea per paragraph.
- Bullets for lists; prose for narrative.
- Open strong, close with a clear next step.
